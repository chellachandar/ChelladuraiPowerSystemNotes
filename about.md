## About the Author

This site is maintained by **Chelladurai Chandarahasan**, a power system
professional with extensive experience in:

- Transmission and EHV/HV substation projects  
- Protection and control engineering  
- Renewable energy integration and grid stability  
- System operation and regulatory compliance  

The content published here reflects **practical engineering understanding**
gained from real-world projects, system studies, commissioning activities,
and grid operation responsibilities.

---

## Purpose of This Platform

**Chelladurai Power System Notes** was created to:

- Document practical power system knowledge in a structured manner  
- Bridge the gap between theory and field application  
- Share engineering lessons that are rarely documented  
- Provide reference tools and explanations for working engineers  

The emphasis is on **engineering logic, operational experience, and
decision-making**, rather than purely academic treatment.

---

## Intended Audience

This platform is intended for:
- Power system and protection engineers  
- Grid operation professionals  
- Researchers and postgraduate students  
- Engineers preparing for real-world responsibilities
